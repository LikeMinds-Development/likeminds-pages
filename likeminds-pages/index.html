Our Mission

LikeMinds is a US 501(c)(3) Nonprofit organization that offers a match-making platform for Youth Entrepreneurs. LikeMinds will provide connections among young entrepreneurs with builders and advisors in order for an idea to transform into a successful startup company. Furthermore, we wish to help new and already running startups to become more successful faster and easier.
